Overview

Italy is considered the birthplace of pizza and is considered one of the best wine-making countries. In this tour you can experience the whole palette of Italian cuisine, taste the best seafood, pasta and traditional pizza with olive oil from the oven. You will also visit one of the oldest wineries and taste classic wine.
